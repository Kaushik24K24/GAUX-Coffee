About Us

GAUX Coffee is a specialty of coffee brand born in the heart of Indore, India. Our journey began with a passion for quality coffee and a desire to share it with the world. We're dedicated to crafting unique, small-batch coffee blends that showcase the best of Indian coffee.

  • Quality: We're obsessed with quality and strive for excellence in every cup.

  • Community: We believe in the power of community and aim to make a positive impact.

  • Innovation: We're always experimenting with new flavors and techniques to push the boundaries of coffee.

  • Sustainability: We're dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint and promoting eco-friendly practices.
